How to make a burger in simple steps!

Welcome to the CIM 111 tutorial for how to make a burger.

Today you will learn how to how to make a burger in simple steps.
Click on the image to begin

  • First thing to do is to go to the store and buy all of your ingridents. That includes meat (or a vegan option) for your burger and any veggies you want. Don't forget a bun and condiments as well!

  • Make your burger patty and chop up all of your veggies so they are ready for plating once you're done.

  • Now you will grill your burger to the temperature of your liking. If you want to make a cheeseburger, wait until the end to add your cheese so it melts!
    Click on the image to see a cheeseburger on the grill!

  • Once your burger is done cooking plate the burger with all of the different toppings that you want.

  • Now after all that hard work, you can eat and enjoy your homemade burger!
